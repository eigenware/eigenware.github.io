welcome to Sri Lanka

‘Aayubowan’ as we greet in Sri Lankan style wishing you ‘long life’ we welcome you and your loved ones to experience the serenity Sri Lanka has to offer during your vacation. We are Ceylon Trends Travels committed at your service to offer you personalized travel and tour packages so that YOU, discover the most diversified cultures in Asia and indulge in Sri Lankan hospitality.

The country was also known by many other names such as Ceylon, Serendib, Taprobane and now the Democratic, Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka. With a total land area of 64,740 km² and 870 km² of water, Sri Lanka stands as a main island in the South of the Indian Ocean. Being rich in many rare natural resources such as rare gems and pearls and diversified with its many human cultures, the Island was known as the Pearl of the Indian Ocean from ancient days when Kings ruled the lands with their prestigious charisma and loyal nobleman.

Today, Sri Lanka ranks among one of the most popular and most affordable destinations in the world as the island has an array of fascinating items to showcase.

You may let go of yourself in the pristine beaches that engulf the island, visit those ancient shrines, water parks and ruins of palaces that were built during King’s reign that still stand majestic in the eyes of the beholder, experience some Hot and Spicy Sri Lankan food cooked to perfection, bond with nature and be amazed at all the exotic wild life you never knew existed or simply enjoy the bliss of Ayurvedic treatment whilst being soaked in a tub filled with aromatic natural oils and lotus flowers.
